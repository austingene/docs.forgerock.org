Chapter 5. Localization

This chapter lists languages and locales supported for OpenAM.

OpenAM console and end user pages are localized for the following languages.

  • English

  • French

  • German

  • Japanese

  • Korean

  • Simplified Chinese

  • Spanish

  • Traditional Chinese
